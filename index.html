Sat Tempo on Incline
5 min warmup. 15 min @ comfortably-hard (75–78% HRmax), 6% incline. 5 min cooldown.
Sun Long Aerobic / Terrain Sim
60 min. Alternate incline 0–8% every 10 min. No pack yet.
Mon Rest
Full rest or 20–30 min easy walk + mobility. Foam roll quads/hips, hip flexor stretch, couch stretch.
